# Electric Vehicle (EV) Dashboard Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project visualizes and analyzes the trends of Electric Vehicle (EV) adoption, sales, and charging infrastructure in the United States. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations to help users understand EV growth patterns, compare different EV models, and study market insights.

## 📝 Problem Statement
The adoption of electric vehicles is growing rapidly. Understanding sales trends, model popularity, and geographical distribution is crucial for manufacturers, policymakers, and consumers. This dashboard aims to provide insights into EV market growth and consumer preferences.

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **Data Visualization:** Tableau  
- **Data Source:** Excel dataset containing EV sales, model details, and charging stations  
- **Dashboard Components:** Line charts, bar charts, tree maps, map charts, pie charts, KPIs  
- **Version Control:** GitHub

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

1. **Total Vehicles by Model Year (From 2010 Onwards)**  
   - **Visualization:** Line / Area Chart  
   - **Description:** Shows EV distribution over the years, helping analyze growth patterns and adoption trends since 2010.

2. **Total Vehicles by State**  
   - **Visualization:** Map Chart  
   - **Description:** Displays the geographical distribution of EVs across states, highlighting regions with higher adoption rates.

3. **Top 10 Total Vehicles by Make**  
   - **Visualization:** Bar Chart  
   - **Description:** Highlights the top 10 EV manufacturers by total vehicles, showing market dominance of specific brands.

4. **Total Vehicles by CAFV Eligibility**  
   - **Visualization:** Pie Chart / Donut Chart  
   - **Description:** Illustrates the proportion of EVs eligible for Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle (CAFV) incentives, showing the impact of incentives on adoption.

5. **Top 10 Total Vehicles by Model**  
   - **Visualization:** Tree Map  
   - **Description:** Highlights the top 10 EV models based on total numbers, giving insights into consumer preferences and popular models.

---

## 📈 Key Metrics & Insights

1. **Total Vehicles**  
   - Understand the overall landscape of EVs, including both BEVs and PHEVs, to assess market size and growth trends.

2. **Average Electric Range**  
   - Shows the average electric range of EVs in the dataset, reflecting technological advancements and efficiency improvements.

3. **Total BEV Vehicles and % of Total BEV Vehicles**  
   - Analyze total Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and their percentage relative to all EVs, indicating the dominance of fully electric models.

4. **Total PHEV Vehicles and % of Total PHEV Vehicles**  
   - Analyze total Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) and their percentage relative to all EVs, showing the market share of hybrid models.

---

## 🔧 Methodology
1. **Data Cleaning:** Handle missing values, remove duplicates.  
2. **Data Transformation:** Aggregate sales and charging station data by year, region, and model.  
3. **Visualization:** Created interactive charts and KPIs in Tableau.  
4. **Analysis:** Identified key trends, top manufacturers/models, and regional adoption patterns.

## ⚠️ Challenges & Limitations
- Incomplete data for some models and regions.  
- Charging station dataset may not include all locations.  
- Real-time updates are not included.

## ✅ Conclusion
The EV Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the electric vehicle market, helping decision-makers understand trends, compare models, and identify infrastructure gaps. Future improvements could include live data integration and predictive analytics for better market forecasting.
